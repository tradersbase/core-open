# TradersBase Features

## Core Analysis Features

### Risk Assessment Algorithm
Our 9-step risk assessment evaluates:

1. **Liquidity Risk** (15 points)
   - Minimum threshold: $1,000
   - Depth across multiple DEXs
   - Trading volume consistency

2. **Holder Concentration** (15 points)
   - Top 10 holder percentage
   - Distribution fairness
   - Whale wallet identification

3. **Creator Balance** (10 points)
   - Creator holdings percentage
   - Token distribution patterns
   - Mint authority status

4. **Market Data** (10 points)
   - Price stability
   - Volume trends
   - Market capitalization

5. **Freeze Authority** (10 points)
   - Authority status check
   - User protection assessment

6. **Mint Authority** (10 points)
   - Supply control verification
   - Inflation risk assessment

7. **Metadata Quality** (10 points)
   - Complete token information
   - Social links presence
   - Logo and description

8. **Age & History** (10 points)
   - Token creation date
   - Historical performance
   - Time in market

9. **Tradability** (10 points)
   - DEX listings count
   - Trading pair availability
   - Market accessibility

**Total Score: 0-100**
- 80-100: Low Risk ✅
- 60-79: Medium Risk ⚠️
- 0-59: High Risk ❌
